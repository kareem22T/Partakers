Abdominoplasty

A tummy tuck is a surgical procedure designed to remove excess skin and fat from the abdominal area. It is often performed on individuals who have experienced significant weight loss or pregnancy, resulting in loose, sagging skin. The procedure can also involve tightening weakened or separated muscles to create a smoother, firmer abdominal profile. The outcome is a more toned and contoured abdomen, enhancing the overall appearance of the body.
