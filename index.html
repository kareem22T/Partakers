Discover our Categories

Aesthetic Surgeries

  • Abdominoplasty: Surgical removal of excess abdominal skin and fat, often combined with muscle tightening.
  • Breast Augmentation: Involves using implants to increase breast size and create a fuller look.
  • Breast Reduction: Reduces the size of the breasts to alleviate discomfort and achieve a more proportionate figure.
  • Breast Reconstruction: Restores the shape of the breast following mastectomy using tissue from other parts of the body or implants.
  • Nasal Surgeries: Includes cosmetic and functional rhinoplasty procedures.
  • Body Contouring: Techniques like liposuction and fat grafting to reshape and sculpt the body.
  • Facial Surgeries: Procedures such as facelift, blepharoplasty, and brow lift.
  • Hair Transplantation: Techniques like FUE for restoring hair and improving volume.

General Surgery

  • Bariatric Surgeries: Includes sleeve gastrectomy, gastric bypass, and adjustable gastric banding.
  • GIT Tumor Surgeries: Procedures like colon resection, liver resection, and Whipple procedure.
  • Thyroid Surgeries: Includes total thyroidectomy and partial thyroidectomy.

Infertility

  • IVF and Related Surgeries: Procedures like egg retrieval, embryo transfer, and hysteroscopy.
  • Male Infertility Surgeries: Includes varicocelectomy, vasectomy reversal, and testicular sperm extraction.

Eye Surgeries

  • Cataract Surgery: Removal of the clouded lens and replacement with an artificial lens.
  • LASIK: Laser procedure to reshape the cornea and correct vision issues.
  • Glaucoma Surgery: Procedures like trabeculectomy or laser therapy to reduce intraocular pressure.
  • Retinal Detachment Surgery: Techniques to reattach the retina and restore vision.

Cardiology

  • PCI (Percutaneous Coronary Intervention): Includes balloon angioplasty and stent placement.
  • Holter Monitoring: Continuous recording of heart activity to detect abnormalities.
  • Pacemaker Implantation: Devices like single-chamber, dual-chamber, and biventricular pacemakers.
  • Echocardiography (ECHO): Imaging techniques like TTE and TEE to visualize heart structure and function.

Renal Patients

  • Renal Dialysis: Includes hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, and CRRT.
  • Kidney Diseases Management: Involves kidney biopsy, chronic kidney disease management, and kidney transplantation.

Dental Surgeries

  • Tooth Extractions: Removal of damaged or infected teeth.
  • Dental Implants: Placement of metal posts to support artificial teeth.
  • Gum Surgery: Treatment for periodontal disease, including gingivectomy.
  • Orthognathic Surgery: Corrective jaw surgery to address skeletal abnormalities.

Medical Consultations

  • General Practice Consultations: Primary care for common illnesses and health management.
  • Specialist Consultations: Evaluations by specialists in various medical fields.
  • Second Opinions: Expert reviews and advice on diagnoses and treatment plans.
  • Telemedicine: Remote consultations providing medical advice without in-person visits.

16 What is an abdominoplasty?

17 What types of breast surgeries are available?

18 What is involved in nasal surgery?

Nasal surgery, or rhinoplasty, can improve both the aesthetics and functionality of the nose, addressing issues such as size, shape, and breathing difficulties.

19 What are bariatric surgeries?
