About Us

Partakers is dedicated to bridging the gap between patients and high-quality medical care in Egypt. Our story began with a vision to make world-class healthcare accessible to everyone. We collaborate with leading medical professionals and state-of-the-art facilities to offer a comprehensive range of medical services. Our mission is to deliver exceptional healthcare experiences, combining medical excellence with compassionate care. We value integrity, innovation, and patient-centricity, striving to enhance the well-being of every individual we serve.

Doctors' Profiles

Dr. Amira Ahmed Nour Eldin

Assistant Professor of Cardiology, Ain Shams University

Dr. Amira Ahmed Nour Eldin is a distinguished cardiologist specializing in congenital and structural heart disease. With extensive experience in pediatric cardiology, she excels in performing and interpreting complex cardiac imaging techniques. Dr. Nour Eldin has a robust background in coronary interventions and structural heart disease management, making her a pivotal figure in both clinical and academic settings

Dr. Mustafa Mohamed Mohamed Abdelmonaem

Associate Professor of Cardiology, Ain Shams University

Dr. Mustafa Mohamed Mohamed Abdelmonaem is a leading cardiologist with profound expertise in managing cardiovascular cases in critical care and cath lab settings. He is renowned for his proficiency in echocardiography and complex interventional cardiology procedures. Dr. Mustafa's commitment to excellence has earned him recognition as a top physician in major hospitals across Egypt.

Dr. Dalia Rashad Mohamed Mahmod

Obstetrician and Gynecologist, IVF Specialist

Dr. Dalia Rashad Mohamed Mahmod is a seasoned obstetrician and gynecologist with a robust focus on IVF and fetal medicine. With extensive experience at various prestigious hospitals, she is adept in managing complex obstetric and gynecological cases. Dr. Dalia’s qualifications include a Master's degree from Ain Shams University and diplomas in IVF and fetal medicine, showcasing her commitment to excellence in reproductive health.

Dr. Osama Esam Eldin Rashad

Lecturer at Bariatric Unit, Ain Shams University

Dr. Osama Esam Eldin Rashad is an experienced surgeon specializing in bariatric and general surgery. His career spans several key positions, including senior residency in endocrine and hepatobiliary units at Ain Shams University Hospital. Dr. Osama has extensive surgical proficiency, having performed numerous complex operations. His qualifications include an MD and Master's degree in surgery from Ain Shams University.

Dr. Mahmoud Attia Shaheen

Plastic Surgery Specialist

Dr. Mahmoud Attia Shaheen is a skilled plastic surgeon with a comprehensive background in plastic and reconstructive surgery. He holds an Egyptian Board of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery certification and has extensive experience working in renowned hospitals, including 6 October Central Hospital and Sheikh Zayed Specialized Hospital. Dr. Shaheen specializes in body contouring, breast surgery, facial cosmetic procedures, and managing plastic surgery emergencies. He is also adept in burn management and post-burn reconstruction.

Dr. Ahmed Hemdan Mohamed

Consultant of Obstetrics and Gynecology

Dr. Ahmed Hemdan Mohamed is a highly experienced consultant in obstetrics and gynecology with over 15 years of practice. He specializes in managing high-risk pregnancies, pain-free natural and cesarean deliveries, and early detection of fetal abnormalities. Dr. Hemdan is proficient in treating recurrent miscarriages, infertility, polycystic ovary syndrome, and uterine fibroids. He also performs IVF, artificial insemination, and various gynecological surgeries, including cosmetic procedures.

Dr. Ihab Wagdi Ragi

Specialist Plastic Surgeon

Dr. Ihab Wagdi Ragi is a specialist in plastic surgery with a Master's degree in the field. He has been practicing since 2015, with experience in Sheikh Zayed Specialized Hospital and his private clinic. Dr. Ragi is dedicated to providing exceptional cosmetic and reconstructive surgery services, ensuring the highest standards of patient care and surgical outcomes.
